about

Felix Šoletić is a Croatian-born, New York-based freelance designer and creative lead with over 9 years of experience. Part graphic designer & typographer, part CG artist & photographer—his versatility allows him to contribute over a wide range of projects. He specializes in pitching & directing title sequences for films, TV shows, and games—frequently extending that aesthetic to the world of commercials, branding, marketing, and experiential projects. He thrives in the beginning stages of a project—developing the idea, finding its look & feel, and turning it into a story.
